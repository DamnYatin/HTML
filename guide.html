Step-by-Step Registration Guide

Follow these steps to successfully register for courses:

  1. Verify Prerequisites: Check the syllabus for any course requirements (e.g., CSC101 is required for CSC202).
  2. Check Enrollment Status: Use the Dashboard to ensure the course has capacity.
  3. Access Student Portal: Log in using your university credentials on the main registration portal.
  4. Add Courses to Cart: Search for the Course IDs (e.g., BUS410) and add them to your schedule.
  5. Finalize Payment: Complete the tuition payment process before the deadline.
